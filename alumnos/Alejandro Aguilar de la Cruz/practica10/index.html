SEDANS

A sedan is a type of passenger car with four doors, a separate trunk, and a classic three-box design. It's a vehicle known for its comfort and interior space, making it ideal for families and long trips. Its aerodynamic structure contributes to good fuel efficiency. In short, the sedan is a versatile and practical option that combines functionality and performance.

SUVS

SUVs (Sports Utility Vehicles) are vehicles with a taller body, more interior space, and a high driving position. They combine the comfort of a passenger car with off-road features, such as all-wheel drive. They are popular for their versatility, ample cargo space, and a sense of security and robustness.

LUXURY

Luxury cars are distinguished by offering superior levels of comfort, equipment, and technology. They use high-quality materials, such as leather and wood, and are meticulously constructed and finished. In addition to their superior performance, they often include advanced safety and entertainment features and represent a symbol of prestige and status.
